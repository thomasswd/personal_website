About Me

Hey there, my name is Thomas Song, and I am a front-end web developer located to the Greater Seattle Area. My main tech stack consists of HTML5, CSS3, vanilla JavaScript (ES6) and React JS. I find that front end development offers a nice mix of working with visual styles and logical problem solving that leaves me with a sense of satisfaction after every project.

Prior to pursuing front end development, I worked in Silicon Valley as an electrical engineer testing high speed signals for server-grade motherboards. Eventually I decided to pursue another path for my career. After spending hours of my free time diving into learning the basics of front end development, I knew that it was the right path for me.

I love bringing beautiful and creative designs to life, as well as building unique websites and applications that can solve any range of problems. I also appreciate how there is always something new to learn in front end development.
